About Schwengle Electronics

Schwengle Electronics specializes in developing hardware applications for automation, control and sensing solutions. Our clients come from various industries, creating specialized machines, innovative material measuring solutions, or real world data harvesting for automation/control end goals. When off the shelf electronics and sensing devices fall short, they turn to us for custom-engineered solutions that meet their unique needs.

Our team of expert engineers have deep technical knowledge and creativity to deliver hardware solutions tailored to demanding applications. We are able to collaborate with innovators to accelerate their R&D, driving innovation forward.
